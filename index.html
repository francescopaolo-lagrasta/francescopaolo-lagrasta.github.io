About

I am a creative before a management engineer

I am a creative homo sapiens: alongside ddadathe result, be it academic or professional, I always hope to have fun and leave my personal stylistic signature on the works I interface with. I have a particular aptitude for challenges , especially those that require choral and multidisciplinary team efforts.

I firmly believe in the values of sustainability and feminism. My academic and professional path aligns with these principles. I have indeed deepened numerous aspects of sustainability in the business field, and I have had the opportunity to investigate, in the context of my PhD, the relationship between feminist values and entrepreneurial behavior.

Throughout my life journey I have engaged in heterogeneous activities, ranging from designing a project for sustainable coffee farming in Tanzania to writing scripts for serious games aimed at educating more responsible citizens and entrepreneurs.
